Quick FAQs

Is there student support if I’m struggling?

Yes. Student support services can help with guidance, direction, and wellbeing. If you’re not sure where to start, contact the college and they will point you to the right service.

What facilities can students use?

Facilities vary by institute and course, but typically include specialised labs, workshops, studios, and learning spaces designed to match your subject area.

How do I find campus information?

You can explore student information pages, check your course details, or contact the college for directions and help.
