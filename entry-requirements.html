General guidance

Entry requirements can vary depending on the course, institute, and MQF level. The points below are a simple overview to help you understand how progression usually works.

  • MQF Level 3diploma level (core skills)

  • MQF Level 5–6higher education pathways

Alternative routes

Some programmes may accept mature students, related experience, or bridging routes. If you’re unsure, contact MCAST and ask for guidance based on your background.

Do I need Maths and English?

Many courses require basic passes in English and/or Maths, especially for progression to higher levels. Requirements vary by programme, so always check the course details.

Can I apply if I’m a mature student?

Some programmes may accept mature students depending on experience and eligibility. If you’re not sure, contact MCAST for guidance.

What if I don’t meet the requirement?

You may have a progression route available, such as starting at a lower MQF level and moving up, or choosing a related programme that matches your current qualifications.
